Porridge Recipe

A quick and simple porridge recipe.

Ingredients

  • 250g quaker oats
  • 125ml water
  • 125ml soya milk
  • 250g frozen raspberries

Steps

  1. pour oats into a bowl
  2. add water and milk
  3. add frozen raspberries
  4. place in microwave for 3 minutes
  5. take out and mix
  6. place back in microwave for 2 minutes
  7. take out and eat
