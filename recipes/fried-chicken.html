Korean Fried Chicken Recipe

A quick and easy Korean fried chicken recipe. Spicy and crunchy with garnishes of spring onion, sesame, and coriander.

Ingredients

  • 1kg chicken wings
  • 2tbs soya sauce
  • 2tbs black vinegar
  • 2tbs gochujang
  • sesame seeds
  • coriander
  • spring onions
  • 500g potato starch
  • 3 eggs
  • seasoning mix

Steps

  1. wash chicen and clear all impurities
  2. crack eggs in a bowl and mix them
  3. put potato strach in a sepaate bowl and add seasoning mis
  4. place chicken first in egg then in flour and leave to rest
  5. fry chicken in air fryer for 20 minites at 180 celcius; take out and leave to rest
  6. in a pan on the stiv mix the gochujang, vinegar, and soya sauce until combines to create a suace
  7. place chicken in the pan and stor until coated
  8. plate chicekn and cover in garnishes of spring onion and coriander
