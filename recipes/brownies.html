Brownie Recipe

A super easy brownie recipe for fudgy dark chocolate brownies. This recipe contains dairy, cocoa, and nuts. It is easy to make and will only take 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 185g butter
  • 185g dark chocolate
  • 85g plain flour
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 50g white chocolate
  • 50g milk chocolate
  • 3 eggs
  • 275g caster sugar

Steps

  1. Cut the butter and chocolate into small cubes and tip into a bowl.
  2. Fill a pan with hot wate and place the bowl on top. Put over a low heat until the butter and chocolate have melted.
  3. Remove the bowl from the pan and Leave to cool
  4. Preheat oven to 180C, and line tin with baking paper.<
  5. Sieve flour and cocoa powder into a separate bowl.
  6. Chop white chocolate and milk chocolate into chunks
  7. Break eggs into a bowl and tip in caster sugar. Whisk the eggs and sugar until they look thick and creamy.
  8. Pour the chocolate mixture over the eggy mousse and fold together.
  9. Resift the cocoa and flour mixture into chocolate bowl and mix in.
  10. Mix in the chocolate chunks, and pour into the tin.
  11. Put in the oven and set timer for 25 mins. Cook until the top is shiny, and then take out of the oven.
  12. Leave to cool and then cut into sqaures
